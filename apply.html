Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the duration of the internship?
This is a 4–6 week virtual internship program, designed to provide flexible learning and practical project experience.
Q2. How do I register for this internship program?
Simply complete the application form and submit your details. Once reviewed, eligible candidates will receive an Internship Offer Letter via email.
Q3. What benefits will I gain from this internship?
Hands-on industry experience in your selected domain, practical knowledge through real-world tasks and projects, resume and portfolio development, LinkedIn profile optimization guidance, and improved communication and workplace readiness skills.
Q4. Is this internship completely virtual?
Yes, this is a 100% virtual/remote internship, allowing you to participate from anywhere.
Q5. Will I receive a certificate after completion?
Yes, all successful interns will receive a verifiable Internship Completion Certificate.
