1About Intern Crowd

Intern Crowd is an Indian ed-tech and career platform that helps students and early professionals build real-world skills through online courses, internships, and mock interviews, and earn verifiable certificates on completion. This Privacy Policy applies to interncrowd.in, all sub-pages, and any related services we offer.

2Information We Collect

We collect information that you choose to give us directly when you use our Services:

CategoryExamples
Identity & contact detailsFull name, email address, phone number, city/state
Academic detailsCollege/university, course of study, year, resume
Enrolment detailsCourse, internship, or mock interview selections and progress
Payment detailsTransaction ID and payment status (processed securely by our payment partner — we do not store card or UPI details)
CommunicationsMessages you send us via contact forms, email, or WhatsApp

We may also receive limited information from partner companies, payment gateways, or verification services when it's relevant to your application or certificate.

3Automatically Collected Data

When you visit our website, certain information is collected automatically:

  • Device & browser data: IP address, browser type and version, operating system, and device type.
  • Usage data: pages visited, time spent on the site, links clicked, and referring/exit pages.
  • Approximate location: derived from your IP address, used only at a city/region level.

This data is collected through cookies, pixels, and analytics tools such as Google Analytics, and helps us understand how visitors use Intern Crowd so we can keep improving it.

4How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your account and course/internship enrolment.
  • Match you with relevant internships and course recommendations.
  • Issue, store, and verify certificates of completion.
  • Process payments and send transaction confirmations.
  • Respond to your queries and provide customer support.
  • Send updates about internship openings, course launches, and offers — you can opt out anytime.
  • Analyse site usage to improve our website, services, and learning content.
  • Detect, prevent, and address fraud, abuse, or technical issues.
  • Comply with applicable legal and regulatory obligations.

5Cookies Policy

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help websites remember information about your visit. Intern Crowd uses cookies to:

  • Keep you signed in and remember your dark/light mode preference.
  • Understand how visitors navigate our site (analytics cookies).
  • Support future advertising features, such as Google AdSense.

You can control or disable cookies anytime through your browser settings; note that some parts of the site may not function as intended if cookies are disabled. For full details, see our dedicated Cookie Policy.

6Google AdSense (Future)

Intern Crowd does not currently display advertisements. This section is included to keep you informed in advance, in case Google AdSense is enabled on the site in the future.

If we enable Google AdSense, third-party vendors, including Google, may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the internet. You will be able to opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings, and this section will be updated with full details before AdSense is activated.

7Firebase

We use Google Firebase to power parts of our website and services, such as authentication, hosting, database storage, and analytics. Firebase may collect technical information like device identifiers, app usage statistics, and crash reports to help us run a reliable and secure platform. Firebase processes this data under Google's own privacy and security practices.

8Third-Party Services

We work with trusted third-party providers to operate Intern Crowd, including:

Google Analytics Google Firebase Payment Gateways Google Forms Email & Hosting Providers

These providers only receive the information necessary to perform their function and are expected to safeguard your data in line with their own privacy policies. We do not sell your personal information to anyone. We may share limited information with hiring partners only when you apply for an internship through our platform, and with government or law enforcement authorities where required by law.

9Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information, including encrypted (HTTPS) connections, secure hosting, and restricted access to personal data on a need-to-know basis. However, no method of transmission or storage over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security of your information.

10Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to provide our Services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. Certificate and enrolment records may be kept longer so that certificates remain verifiable. You may request deletion of your data at any time, subject to any legal or record-keeping requirements.

11Children's Privacy

Our Services are intended for students aged 16 and above.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can promptly remove it.

12External Links

Our website may contain links to external sites, such as partner job boards, social media pages, or Google Forms, that are not operated by us. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these third-party sites, and we encourage you to review their privacy policies before sharing any information with them.

13User Rights

Depending on applicable law, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Delete your personal information, subject to legal retention requirements.
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time.
  • Object to certain uses of your data, such as analytics or marketing.

To exercise any of these rights, write to us at hello@interncrowd.in. You may also unsubscribe from marketing emails using the link provided in those emails.

14Changes to Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised "Effective Date". We encourage you to review this page periodically; continued use of our Services after changes means you accept the updated policy.
