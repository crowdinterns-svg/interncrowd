Intern Crowd ("we", "us", "our") operates interncrowd.in and provides internship placements, online courses, mock interviews, and related learning services (the "Services"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using our Services, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Information you provide: name, email address, phone number, college/university, resume, educational details, and payment information when you register for a course, internship, or mock interview.
  • Information collected automatically: IP address, browser type, device information, pages visited, and time spent on our site, collected through cookies and similar technologies.
  • Information from third parties: details shared by partner companies, payment gateways, or verification services when relevant to your application or certificate.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your account and enrolment.
  • Match you with relevant internships and course recommendations.
  • Issue and verify certificates.
  • Process payments and send transaction confirmations.
  • Send updates about internship openings, course launches, and offers (you may opt out at any time).
  • Improve our website, services, and learning content.
  • Comply with legal obligations and prevent fraud or misuse.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Hiring partners or companies, only when you apply for an internship through our platform.
  • Service providers who help us operate our platform, such as payment processors and hosting providers.
  • Government or law enforcement authorities, where required by law.

4. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to keep you signed in, remember your preferences, and understand how our site is used. You can control cookies through your browser settings. For more detail, please see our Cookie Policy.

5. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information, including encrypted connections and restricted access to personal data. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights & Choices

You may request to access, correct, update, or delete your personal information by writing to us at hello@interncrowd.in. You may also unsubscribe from marketing emails using the link provided in those emails.

7. Children's Privacy

Our Services are intended for students aged 16 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date. Continued use of our Services after changes means you accept the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out to us at hello@interncrowd.in or visit www.interncrowd.in.
